Ninja Gauntlet

"Ninja Gauntlet is pure genius" - an AER

Ninja Gauntlet is the last LbcLab game release. Let's discover the new fantasy-themed hack and slash. The new generation of multi-player dungeon crawler !


Neither great generals not shogun, these warriors have been distinguished by their courage, perseverance or even their sometimes trafic fate.
No one can remain marble in front of the will and their determination which illustrates these men. Still less when we face Saigo Takamori...


The Gauntlet saga continues. Release on 18th June 2017

Artificial intelligence devkit

Discover our AI developpement kit

During your Ninja Gauntlet experience, you might play with bots. These artificial intelligeance have been implemented in a way that you can be a full actor of their behaviour. In fact, the AI turns are decided by lua scripts.

We are provinding the players of Ninja Gauntlet a bunch of software and documentation that would help him during his AI developpement process. On the Github page of the devkit, you'll find all the needed informations.
